Executive Summary
Quartz Core: Automated ingest, culling, sync, and assembly of wedding-day footage into three separate, structured XML sequences (ceremony, reception/speeches, long-form highlight) compatible with Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, and Final Cut Pro.

• Quartz Social (+$20/month): Automated vertical reel generation from finished highlights, with AI-generated captions and optional agent-assisted posting to Instagram and TikTok for marketing purposes.

The AI video editing market is experiencing rapid growth, projected to reach $9.3 billion by 2033 with a CAGR of approximately 30-31%. Within this broader market, wedding-specific video editing represents a high-value niche.

1. Market Landscape & Competitive Positioning
1.1 Current Market State
• Professional videographers shooting 2-6 weddings per month on average.
• Editing time of 10-20 hours per wedding for traditional workflows.
• Outsourcing costs ranging from $250-$400 per wedding for basic edits, up to $1,800-$3,500 for full cinematic packages.
• Growing adoption of AI tools for photo culling and editing, with video automation following similar trajectory.

1.2 Competitive Landscape
Quartz occupies white space as the wedding-native AI assistant editor that delivers end-to-end automation from card ingest through structured NLE-ready timelines.

1.3 Quartz's Unique Position
1. Wedding-aware semantics: Explicit detection and segmentation of prep, first look, ceremony, vows, speeches, first dance, parent dances, reception, and exit sequences.
2. Multi-sequence XML output: Three separate, editable timelines (ceremony, reception, highlight) exported directly to user's preferred NLE.
3. 80% completion philosophy: Structure, sync, culling, and obvious trims handled by AI; creative decisions, music selection, color, and pacing left to editor.
4. Per-creator style learning: System learns individual shooter's pacing, shot selection, and color preferences across multiple past films.
5. Built-in marketing layer: Unique social reel generation from finished highlights specifically for videographer brand promotion.

2. Product Specifications
2.1 Quartz Core Features
2.1.1 Ingest & Sync
• Multi-source ingest: Accepts footage from multiple camera cards, external audio recorders, drone footage, and secondary cameras.
• Automatic sync: Waveform analysis handles audio drift and multi-camera synchronization automatically.
• Metadata preservation: Maintains timecode, frame rates, and camera identifiers throughout processing.

2.1.2 AI Culling & Selection
• Shot quality analysis: Identifies and removes footage with focus issues, extreme shake, dead time, and unusable audio.
• Moment detection: Finds emotionally significant moments using facial recognition, audio analysis, and motion patterns.
• Best-take selection: When multiple angles capture same moment, selects best composition and framing.

2.1.3 Timeline Assembly
• Quartz generates three distinct XML sequences per wedding: Ceremony, Reception/Speeches, and Long Highlight.

2.1.4 NLE Integration
• Export formats: XML for Adobe Premiere Pro, XML for DaVinci Resolve, FCPXML for Final Cut Pro.

2.2 Quartz Social (+$20/month Add-On)
• Reel Generation: 5-10 vertical reels per wedding, 15-45 seconds.
• AI Caption & Hashtag Generation: Wedding-specific voice.
• Delivery Modes: Local Export or Agent-Assisted Posting (OpenClaw).
